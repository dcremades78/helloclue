01 · The Opportunity

In women's health, trust is the whole game.

Nobody downloads a cycle app on a whim. Women's health is one of the most personal categories there is — users hand over the most intimate data they have, so they download what they trust. And the most trusted channel on social isn't an ad; it's a creator a woman already follows, talking honestly about her body. Clue has 10M+ users, a million paying subscribers and something almost no competitor can claim: a privacy-first, European, GDPR-grade brand that doesn't sell user data. That's not just a value — it's a story creators can tell. What's missing is the engine that turns "I trust this app" into trackable installs and Clue Plus subscribers, run natively in each market.

The trust wedge nobody else has

In the US especially, women are newly anxious about where their cycle data goes. Clue's stance — data stored in Europe under GDPR, never sold, subscriptions that fund research — is exactly the reassurance the category is searching for. Creators can say what an ad can't: "this is the one I actually trust with my data."

Two markets, two different jobs

The same playbook does a different thing in each market. In the US it scales the largest, highest-value women's health market on earth. In Germany it defends and deepens Clue's home base, in a market that prizes Datenschutz more than almost anywhere.

02 · The Strategy

Don't bet on one creator size. Layer them.

The most common mistake is spending a whole budget on one big name — or only on cheap nano creators. Each tier does a different job. Combined, they cover the full funnel: reach and credibility at the top, conversion in the middle, authenticity and volume at the base. We build this same pyramid in each market, casting locally across women's health, OB-GYN-adjacent, wellness and lifestyle niches.

1

Cast across tiers

Ramped sources a mix of nano, micro and mid creators in women's health, cycle, fertility, wellness and lifestyle — native to each market, briefed for compliance.

2

Brief & create

Each creator lives a real Clue habit — tracking a cycle, logging symptoms, learning a pattern — and produces authentic content, with paid usage rights included.

3

Concentrated launch

Creators post in the same window. The install spike lifts App Store ranking in Health & Fitness — extra organic installs on top.

4

Amplify winners

Top organic videos are boosted as paid ads. You spend media budget only on creative already proven to convert.

Why combine sizes (and not just go big)

A single big creator gives you one shot, one audience, one CPM. Ten layered creators give you ten audiences, ten content angles to test, and a portfolio that de-risks the campaign. If one underperforms, the budget moves — you're never all-in on a single bet.

Why layering drives more reach

Mid-tier health voices create the awareness wave and credibility; micro creators deliver the best engagement and lowest cost per install; nano creators add authentic volume and tight-knit cycle and TTC communities. Women who see the same app from several creators they trust convert far better than from one big post.

05 · The Original Idea

The two markets aren't two campaigns. They're one engine.

Here's the move most agencies miss. The US and Germany share the same buyer — a woman who wants to understand her body and trust the app that helps her. So the winning trust-led hooks, the strongest creators' formats and the Spark-ready UGC don't stay in one market. We treat the two as a single learning system: each market plays a role, and the creative learning compounds across both.

🇺🇸 Scale & test in the US

The biggest women's health market on earth — and the sharpest place to prove the trust angle. The US generates the most data, the most winning hooks and the largest Spark-ready content library, fast.

🇩🇪 Deepen the home market in Germany

The proven, trust-led creative lands in Clue's highest-LTV home base — a market that prizes Datenschutz, where the privacy story isn't a nice-to-have, it's the headline.

♻️ One shared content library

Every winning angle and licensed asset feeds both markets' paid social. You build a reusable, performance-tested UGC library once — and amplify it everywhere it works.

The result: learning compounds across borders. Run alone, each market is a standalone buy. Run together, the US de-risks Germany and the trust-led creative library serves both. You learn faster, and you only put paid budget behind creative that's already proven to convert.

07 · Guarantee & Terms

The risk sits with us, not with you.

This is what makes the model safe to try in either market: you don't pre-fund anything, you only pay for work that actually goes live, and every campaign carries a guaranteed view commitment.

Guaranteed views

  • We agree a minimum number of views per campaign up front — so performance is committed, not hoped for.
  • If a creator's content doesn't hit the agreed views, we reshoot or repost until it does.
  • If it still falls short, you don't pay for that creator. The view floor protects your spend.
  • Guaranteed views give you a known reach baseline to plan installs and subscribers around.

How you pay — no upfront risk

  • Invoiced at the end of each month — never before.
  • You only pay for content that has actually been published. Posts that don't go live aren't billed.
